Blitz

Blitz is the Seahawks main mascot. He also has a sidekick named Boom. Blitz was born on September 13, 1998. According to the Seahawks website, his hobbies are reading, fitness and birdwatching.

The Seattle Seahawks are a professional football team in the National Football League. They won a Superbowl two seasons ago. Some of their best players include Marshawn Lynch, Russell Wilson, Jimmy Graham, Kam Chancelor, Richard Sherman, Michael Bennett, and Earl Thomas. Ty Lockett has been their star rookie so far this season as he has returned a punt and kickoff for a touchdown.
